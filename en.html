Privacy Policy

Right to Ask (حقنا نسأل) — Citizen-Municipality Communication Platform

Summary: We only collect data necessary to operate the app. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Right to Ask (حقنا نسأل). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

  • Survey responses

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service Delivery: Display news, projects, and services related to your municipality
  • Complaint Processing: Forward your complaints and suggestions to the relevant municipality
  • Notifications: Send updates about your complaints, news, and events
  • Service Improvement: Analyze aggregated, anonymized data to improve municipal services
  • Identity Verification: Send OTP codes to verify phone number ownership

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • Your Municipality: Complaints and suggestions are sent to your municipality for follow-up
  • Legal Obligation: If required by law or court order

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on secured servers
  • Communication between the app and server is encrypted
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted using Keychain (iOS) and Keystore (Android)
  • Passwords are hashed and cannot be viewed

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View your personal data through your profile in the app
  • Modification: Update your information at any time from the Settings screen
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account from within the app (Settings → Delete Account). All personal data will be anonymized
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications from the app settings

8. Children

Right to Ask is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that we have collected a child's data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any material changes via an in-app notification. Your continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the changes.
